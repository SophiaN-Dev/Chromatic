Chromatic — Privacy Policy ✿

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Chromatic is a color toolkit browser extension created by Sophia N. Your privacy matters, and Chromatic is designed to respect it completely.

Data Collection

Chromatic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

Local Storage

Chromatic saves your preferences (saved colors, palettes, theme choice) locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone else — including us.

Image Processing

When you use the Image Extract feature, all processing happens entirely within your browser using the HTML Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere. They stay on your device.

EyeDropper

The color picker uses Chrome's EyeDropper API to sample colors from your screen. This requires the "activeTab" permission. No screenshot is taken or stored — only the single color value you pick is captured.

Analytics & Tracking

Chromatic does not use any analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, or third-party services. There are no ads.

Third Parties

Chromatic does not share any data with third parties because there is no data to share.

Permissions Explained

activeTab — Needed for the EyeDropper API to pick colors from the webpage you're viewing.

storage — Needed to save your color collections and preferences locally in your browser.
